Frequently Asked Questions

▼
How do you dispose of pet waste?
We treat waste disposal seriously because your pet's health matters. Dog waste requires proper handling, so we double-bag everything in eco-friendly bags and secure it in your trash can. We can also place it beside your home if you prefer. This careful approach protects your family and the environment.
How do you keep my dog safe during service?
Your dog's safety is everything to us. Between every yard visit, we clean and disinfect all equipment and footwear with kennel-grade, eco-friendly disinfectant. This stops any germs from spreading between homes. After we finish, we send you gate photos so you can confirm your pups are safe and sound. That's the level of care you get with every visit.
Can you clean with my dog in the yard?
For everyone's safety, we ask that dogs stay indoors while we work. This keeps your pet protected and lets our team work efficiently and safely. If dogs are left outside repeatedly, we may need to stop service at that property. We appreciate your help in keeping everyone safe.
Why is She Scoops premium priced?
You're paying for quality, not just convenience. We background-check our team, carry full insurance, use eco-friendly methods, provide gate photos, and guarantee 100% satisfaction. Every booking also supports local dog rescue. Our 50+ customers tell us it's worth it, and we'd rather do exceptional work for the right customers than cut corners to compete on price.
Why should I support a female-owned business?
Research shows female-owned businesses consistently prioritize customer satisfaction and community impact. That's exactly what we do. Your support helps us grow and build more opportunities in Santa Clarita.
How does the rescue partnership work?
We partner with local 501(c)(3) dog rescue organizations and donate from every booking. That money rescues abused and neglected dogs, pays for medical care, and helps them find homes. We've already donated over $2,400. When you book with us, you're actually saving dog lives.
