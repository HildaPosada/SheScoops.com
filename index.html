Frequently Asked Questions

How do you dispose of pet waste?
Your pet's health and environmental safety are our top priorities. Dog waste is classified as a hazardous material and must be disposed of properly. We double-bag all waste using eco-friendly bags and place it securely in your trash can. If preferred, we can position it neatly beside your residence. This ensures safe, sanitary disposal that protects both your family and the environment.
How do you keep my dog safe during service?
Safety is our highest priority. We thoroughly clean and disinfect all equipment and footwear with eco-friendly, kennel-grade disinfectant between every single yard visit. This prevents any potential germ transmission between properties. After completing our service, we provide gate photos for your peace of mind, confirming your pets are safe and secure within the yard. Your dog's health and well-being drive everything we do.
Can you clean with my dog in the yard?
For the safety of both your beloved pet and our professional team, we kindly require that all dogs remain indoors during the cleaning process. This allows us to work efficiently and accurately while ensuring no one gets hurt. The safety of our team members is paramountâ€”if dogs are repeatedly left outside during scheduled visits, we may need to remove the property from our service route. We appreciate your cooperation in keeping everyone safe!
